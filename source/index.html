Hello, world!

My name is Maria, I am 27 years old eager and passionate user experience designer with background in graphic and web design.

I have been studying web design for more than two years. For one year I studied UI/UX design courses from Mail.ru Group, then I studied design on my own for a year. In fact, my training does not end until now. I try to constantly improve my qualifications and learn something new.

I worked in a design studio for six months, and I work freelance for six months. Now I looking for next role within an web/app or product design space to ultilise my design skills and enhance user experiences within mobile, tablet and web-based devices.

I am fond of visual art - I paint and photograph. I like to travel, read books and expand my mind.
